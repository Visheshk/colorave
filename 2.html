What can you understand about the system from seeing the depicted behavior above?

The aim was to enable a description of multiples waves, and seeing what color this mixture corresponds to. I discussed with more friends of mine, about the description of color, and some people who were well versed with the Fourier Transform, and wave analysis, told me that wave-things like a 'color', or a 'sound', as we perceive it, is a mixture of numerous waves, and it is best represented on a frequency-intensity graph.

So what you're seeing is the fiducials controlling the frequency intensity representation of the light wave, changed from the earlier control over the waveform's configuration. And the beam of color at the bottom is the total color obtained. And the numbers at the bottom being the RGB values of the total color.
